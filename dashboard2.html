About Our Barangay System

Our Mission

To provide an efficient digital platform that connects barangay officials and residents, streamlining community services and improving governance transparency.

Key Features

  • Secure user accounts for all barangay stakeholders
  • Role-based access control (Residents, KAGAWAD, Chairman, Admin)
  • Document request and tracking system
  • Community announcements and notifications
  • Complaint and suggestion management
